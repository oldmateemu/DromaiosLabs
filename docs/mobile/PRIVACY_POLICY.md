## 1. Who we are

Dromaios Safer Practice ("the app") is provided by Dromaios Labs ("we", "us", "our"), an
Australian healthcare innovation company. You can contact us about privacy at
**[privacy@dromaios.com]**.

## 2. The short version

- The app is **free** and you can use it **without creating an account**.
- Your learning record (lessons completed, certificates, hours) is **yours** and is stored
  **on your own device**.
- We do **not** share your learning record with your employer. The app is **not** a
  monitoring or compliance-checking tool used against you.
- We collect **as little as possible**.
- We do **not** sell your personal information.

## 3. What we collect, and why

**Information you give us**
- **Your work role** (e.g. disability support, aged care, nurse, or "prefer not to say").
  Used only to frame the learning scenarios. Stored on your device.

**Information stored on your device**
- Your **learning record**: which lessons you've completed, certificates issued, and hours
  recorded. This stays on your device unless and until you choose to create an account and
  sync it (account sync is not available in this version).

**Information collected automatically**
- We aim to keep this minimal. If we use diagnostic or crash-reporting tools to keep the app
  stable, they collect technical information (such as device type, app version and error
  data). [Confirm exactly which tool(s), if any, are used at launch and list them here, e.g.
  Expo/Sentry, and link their policies.] We do not use this to identify you personally.
- [State clearly whether any analytics are used. If none, say "We do not use third-party
  advertising or marketing analytics."]

We do **not** collect health information about the people you support through this app.

## 4. How we use your information

- To provide the app and your learning experience.
- To keep your private learning record and issue certificates.
- To keep the app working, secure and stable.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell it.

## 5. Disclosure

- We do **not** disclose your learning record to your employer.
- We only disclose personal information to service providers who help us run the app (for
  example, hosting or crash diagnostics), and only as needed for those purposes.
- We may disclose information if required by law.

## 6. Where your data is stored (data residency)

- Your learning record is stored **on your device**.
- Where we process personal information on our servers (for example, if you later create an
  account to sync), we will use **Australian-based hosting** where practicable. If any
  information is processed overseas, we will take reasonable steps as required by Australian
  Privacy Principle 8 before any such disclosure. [Confirm and name regions/providers at
  launch.]

## 7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised
access. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but keeping your record on
your device by default reduces the data we hold about you.

## 8. Retention

Your on-device record stays until you clear it (you can clear it any time in "My record") or
uninstall the app. [If/when account sync exists, describe server-side retention.]

## 9. Children

The app is intended for adult workers and is not directed at children.

## 10. Your rights

You can:
- Access and clear your on-device learning record at any time within the app.
- Contact us at **[privacy@dromaios.com]** to ask about the personal information we hold (if
  any) and to request access or correction.
- Complain to us, and if you're not satisfied, to the **Office of the Australian Information
  Commissioner (OAIC)** at oaic.gov.au.

## 11. A note on the content

The app is **educational**. It supports your knowledge, confidence and awareness of good
practice and your obligations. It is **not** clinical, legal or behaviour-support advice, and
it is **not** proven to reduce incidents, violence or the use of restrictive practices. In an
emergency, prioritise safety and follow your organisation's procedures.

## 12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We'll change the "Last updated" date and, for significant changes,
provide a more prominent notice.
