## Full description

**Practical skills for the hardest moments of care work.**

Dromaios Safer Practice gives frontline disability, aged-care and nursing workers short,
realistic lessons on de-escalation and behaviour support — the kind of situations you actually
face, not theory. It's free, and it's built for real shifts: lessons take a few minutes and
work on your own phone.

**Learn through realistic scenarios.**
Each lesson puts you in a real-feeling situation and lets you choose how to respond, with clear,
respectful feedback that explains the thinking — so you build judgement and confidence, not just
tick a box.

**Quick reference when you need it.**
Skim practical guidance in the moment: early signs of escalation, calm-communication prompts,
least-restrictive principles, and a reminder of what kinds of incidents need reporting. Works
offline.

**A record that's yours.**
Finish a lesson and save a dated certificate to your private record, with the hours you've put
in. It stays on your device — it is not shared with your employer — and you can use it as
evidence toward your CPD where you have a CPD obligation.

**Built by Dromaios Labs.**
We build practical healthcare education and tools close to the realities of frontline care.

**Important:** This app is educational. It supports your knowledge, confidence and awareness of
good practice and your obligations. It is not clinical, legal or behaviour-support advice, and
it is not a substitute for your organisation's policies, a person's behaviour support plan, or
professional supervision. In an emergency, prioritise safety and follow your organisation's
procedures.
