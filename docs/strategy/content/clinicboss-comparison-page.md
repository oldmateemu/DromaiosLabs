## Where ClinicBoss is built to be different (draft positioning — keep claims earned)

These are the *gaps* the table above points to. Frame as the problems we're designed around, not as proven outcomes (per guardrails):

1. **Compliance and accreditation built into the daily workflow — not bolted on.** Today, practice-management software, your training/LMS, and your accreditation/audit evidence live in three separate places, stitched together by hand before an audit. ClinicBoss is designed so the evidence trail (training completions, incidents, policies) maps to the actual standards as you work.
2. **You own your data — one-click, audit-ready export.** Some incumbents make it genuinely hard to get your full records out. Easy export isn't a feature to us; it's a principle.
3. **Australian-hosted, with human, Australian-timezone support.** Support quality is the single most common complaint across this market. We treat it as core, not overhead.
4. **Transparent, predictable pricing.** No surprise credit balances, no per-seat penalty for growing your team. You should be able to predict your bill.
5. **Built for the aged-care + NDIS + allied-health reality**, including the 2025 strengthened standards — not retrofitted from a generalist tool.

> **Guardrail reminder:** keep #1–#5 at problem/principle level until pilots and evidence exist. Avoid "compliant by design," outcome percentages, "trusted by clinics," or final-brand certainty for ClinicBoss. Soften any direct competitor criticism to neutral, factual statements.
