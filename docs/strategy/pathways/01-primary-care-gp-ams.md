# Pathway 01 — Primary Care: Community Clinics / GPs + AMS / ACCHOs

> Grouped because both run on the **RACGP Standards for general practices (5th ed.)** clinical
> accreditation pathway (AGPAL or QPA, 3-year cycle), and accreditation is the
> **gate to funding** (PIP, bulk-billing incentives). AMS/ACCHOs are treated as an **extension**
> of the GP pathway with three additions: organisational accreditation (QIC/ISO), funding-linked
> **nKPI/OSR** reporting, and **Indigenous Data Sovereignty**.
>
> Cross-references: `../clinicboss-module-catalogue.md` (V-GP, V-AMS) and
> `../dromaiosed-course-catalogue.md` (F03, U02, S07, F05, F01).

## A. Snapshot

### A1. Community clinics / GPs
- **~40,375 GPs** in the primary-care workforce (2024); projected shortfall **7,700 GPs by 2033**.
- **~7,000 general practices** (no authoritative register; range ~6,500–7,200). ~7,135 accredited
  practices commonly cited.
- **>22M patients, >169M GP services (2023)**; ~9 in 10 Australians see a GP yearly.
- **Ownership:** corporatising — 45% of GPs in company-owned practices, ~20% in corporate groups
  (10+ sites); only ~2% solo. Owner aspiration falling.
- **Economic buyer:** **Practice Manager** (operational: compliance, training, accreditation,
  software) → **Principal/owner or partners** (capital, clinical software) → corporate ops for
  chains. Peak body for managers: **AAPM** (CPM credential).

### A2. AMS / ACCHOs
- **~148 ACCHOs** (NACCHO members), **>550 sites**, **>3.6M episodes/yr** to **~410,000 people**.
- **~6,000–7,000 staff**, ~54–56% Aboriginal/Torres Strait Islander (3rd-largest First Nations
  employer). Reported workforce strain since 2020 (flag: COVID-era data).
- **Community-controlled**, elected boards; coordinated by **NACCHO + state affiliates** (AH&MRC,
  VACCHO, AHCSA, QAIHC). Less fragmented than mainstream GP.
- **Economic buyer:** **ACCHO CEO** + **community-elected Board** (governance sign-off) +
  clinical/quality & practice managers. NACCHO/affiliates = collective procurement & channel.

## B. Regulatory drivers (what forces spend)

- **RACGP Standards 5th ed.** — five areas (communication & participation; rights & needs;
  governance & risk; comprehensive care; clinical safety/quality). **3-year accreditation** +
  assessment scheduled 4–8 months before expiry; self-assessment + document review + on-site
  assessor (a mid-cycle/mid-point review is a *proposed* ACSQHC change, **not** the current baseline).
  Agencies:
  **AGPAL, QPA** (QIP in adjacent sectors).
- **Mandatory operational requirements:** CPR currency, IPC, cold-chain/vaccine-fridge logs,
  anaphylaxis/emergency, sterilisation/reprocessing, privacy/records, complaints & incident
  management, recall/reminder systems, policy & procedure manuals.
- **CPD (AHPRA/Medical Board, since 2023):** **50 hrs/yr** (≥12.5 educational; ≥25 across
  reviewing performance + measuring outcomes; ≥5 reflecting on feedback) + annual PDP + CPR per
  triennium; tracked via RACGP **MyCPD**/ACRRM.
- **Accreditation is financially mandatory** — it gates **PIP** and most MyMedicare-linked
  incentives. *Caveat:* the **Bulk Billing Practice Incentive Program (BBPIP, from 1 Nov 2025)** has an
  **accreditation exemption** for practices registering in MyMedicare for the first time *purely* for
  BBPIP — so don't frame accreditation as the gate to *all* bulk-billing incentives (PIP still is).
- **AMS additions:** **dual/multi-accreditation** (RACGP clinical + **QIC Health & Community
  Services Standards** or **ISO 9001** organisational); **nKPI** (20 indicators, 6-monthly) +
  **OSR** (annual) reporting, both tied to IAHP funding; cultural-safety training; Closing-the-Gap
  data fields.

## C. Funding levers (the budget that pays us)

- **PIP** (Quality, Capacity, Rural), paid quarterly. **PIP QI Incentive: A$5/SWPE/yr, capped
  A$12,500/quarter** (req. accreditation + quarterly QI data to PHN).
- **Workforce Incentive Program (WIP)** — nurses, ATSI Health Workers, allied health, rural.
- **Bulk Billing Practice Incentive (BBPIP) from 1 Nov 2025** — extra **12.5% MBS loading** for
  fully-bulk-billing practices; part of A$7.9bn Strengthening Medicare.
- **Strengthening Medicare GP Grants** (2023–24, now closed): one-off **A$25k/$35k/$50k** for
  digital uplift, IPC, accreditation — demonstrates a *dedicated compliance/digital budget* exists.
- **AMS:** **Indigenous Australians' Health Programme (IAHP)** — core funding (up to **A$2.697bn
  over 4 yrs from 2024–25**), allocated by client numbers + episodes (via OSR); plus PIP/WIP/MBS
  and PIP Indigenous Health Incentive. Blended grant + Medicare model.

**WTP signal:** GP Grants ($25–50k) + QI PIP (up to $50k/yr) + IAHP grants = real, government-funded
budget lines for exactly what we sell.

## D. Market fit — why Dromaios wins here

The buyer's pain is **accreditation + CPD + mandatory-training evidence**, recurring every cycle,
and it directly protects revenue (PIP, bulk-billing, IAHP). Incumbents are **clinical systems**
(Best Practice ~60%, MedicalDirector, Communicare in >70% of AMS) and an LMS (**Ausmed**) — but the
**white space is the evidence/accreditation-readiness layer** that stitches training + policy +
incidents to the RACGP Standards (and, for AMS, automates nKPI/OSR). We don't displace BP or
Communicare — we **integrate** (FHIR/Halo Connect) and own the audit-readiness job.

## E. Entry wedge

- **GP/community:** **Education-led opener → accreditation-pack anchor.** Start with a low-cost,
  CPD-accredited **IPC (U02)** and **OVA (F01)** course sold to the Practice Manager (short cycle,
  budgeted), then expand to the **"Accreditation-Ready: RACGP 5th ed" (F03)** course + **ClinicBoss
  V-GP** accreditation pack ahead of each practice's 3-year accreditation milestone.
- **AMS:** **Education-led on trust.** Lead with **Cultural Safety (F05, co-designed with First
  Nations clinicians)** + **OVA (F01)**, via **NACCHO/affiliates**, then expand to **ClinicBoss
  V-AMS** (nKPI/OSR automation + dual-accreditation crosswalk) — the highest-value, funding-linked
  pain.

**Required net-new functions for this pathway:** RACGP 5th-ed crosswalk; vaccine-fridge logging;
recall/reminder safety register; PIP-QI extract; nKPI/OSR report builders; FHIR integration to
BP/Communicare; Indigenous data-governance controls.

## H. Pricing & packaging

- **"Accreditation + Capability" bundle:** ClinicBoss core (C2/C3/C4/C8) + DromaiosEd catalogue,
  **per-practice/month** (small clinics) or **flat-unlimited/yr** for corporate groups/ACCHO

## I. Channel & sales motion

- **PHNs (31)** — primary channel: they commission QI/digital/accreditation support and amplify to
  member practices; pursue funded pilots.
- **AAPM** — sell to practice managers (the buyer); National Conference; CPM community.
- **RACGP GP26 + Practice Owners Conference**, **ACRRM Rural Medicine** — credibility + owners.
- **AMS:** **NACCHO + state affiliates** (AH&MRC, VACCHO, AHCSA, QAIHC) — relationship-led,
  collective procurement, cultural endorsement; PHN First-Nations commissioning.
- **Integration partnerships** with Best Practice / Communicare (Halo Connect/FHIR) for
  distribution and stickiness.
- Sales cycle: education/compliance is **short** (PM-led, budgeted); clinical-software switching is
  long — so we *don't* compete there, we attach.

## J. Strategy to move through the stream

1. **Beachhead (M0–6):** CPD-accredited IPC + OVA eLearning to GP practice managers via 1–2 PHNs;
   publish the RACGP 5th-ed Accreditation-Ready course. Win 5–10 reference practices.
2. **Attach ClinicBoss (M3–9):** convert education customers to the "Accreditation + Capability"
   bundle ahead of their 3-year accreditation milestones; ship V-GP + PIP-QI extract.
3. **AMS extension (M6–15):** enter via NACCHO/affiliate cultural-safety + OVA training; co-design

   Indigenous data governance.
4. **Group/corporate (M12+):** flat-unlimited deals to corporate GP groups (IPN/ForHealth-type) and
   ACCHO networks; integration with BP/Communicare as the expansion lever.
5. **Land-and-expand metric:** education seats → bundle conversion rate; practices "audit-ready"
   score; nKPI/OSR hours saved per ACCHO.

## K. AI plays & guardrails

- **Plays:** AI **PIP-QI and nKPI/OSR report drafting** from CIS data (human sign-off); accreditation
  **evidence assembler** mapped to RACGP 5th ed; **policy generator** (state-localised); recall
  list QA. Integrate (not build) ambient scribes (Heidi/Lyrebird — ~22% GP adoption, Lyrebird free
  in BP) for the documentation-burden story.
- **Guardrails:** non-SaMD (no diagnosis/treatment suggestion); onshore data; APP/My Health Record
  compliant; scribe use needs documented patient consent + GP oversight (AHPRA/RACGP Aug-2024);
  **AMS: Indigenous Data Sovereignty** and community data control are decisive for trust.

## L. Risks & competitive notes

- **Incumbent clinical-system lock-in** (BP, Communicare) — mitigate by integrating, not replacing.
- **Ausmed** owns GP LMS mindshare — beat on CPD-accredited flagships + bundled accreditation
  evidence + price.
- **AMS trust barrier** — must be relationship- and community-led; cultural-safety credibility and
  Indigenous data governance are non-negotiable; co-design with First Nations clinicians.
- **No authoritative practice register** — pipeline sizing via PHN partnerships, not lists.
