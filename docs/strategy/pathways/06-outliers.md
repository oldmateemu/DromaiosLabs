# Pathway 06 — Outliers: Dental, Pharmacy, Optometry, Justice, Defence, Telehealth & Niches

> The diverse "everything else" of healthcare. Each is a distinct niche, but they share the
> non-discriminatory doctrine: **same engine, thin vertical pack, peak-body/banner-group channel.**
> **Wave 3** — opportunistic, channel-led, often via franchise/banner HQs (one sale → many sites)
> or a sharp accreditation deadline (pharmacy QCPP→QSPP). Day surgeries are covered in Pathway 04
> (shared NSQHS), not here.
>
> Cross-references: `../clinicboss-module-catalogue.md` (V-PHARM, V-DENT, V-OVA) and
> `../dromaiosed-course-catalogue.md` (S11, S12, F03, F01, U02).

## B. Regulatory drivers (by niche)

- **Dental:** AHPRA + CPD; **NSQHS mandatory for licensed day-procedure** (sedation/surgical) dental;
  ADA infection-control guidelines; state radiation-apparatus licensing.
- **Pharmacy:** Pharmacy Board/AHPRA; **QCPP (Quality Care Pharmacy Program) → QSPP** transition
  (QCPP elements phased out by Sep 2029); ownership rules (e.g. QLD Pharmacy Business Ownership Act
  2024). **8CPA** (from 1 Jul 2024; A$26.5bn/5yr incl. DAAs, MedsChecks, staged supply) funds
  professional services that need documentation/claiming.
- **Optometry:** Optometry Board/AHPRA CPD; therapeutic endorsement; teleoptometry expansion (OPSM/
  Specsavers remote models) creates compliance/CPD-tracking demand.
- **Correctional:** **RACGP Standards for health services in Australian prisons (2nd ed.)** + NSQHS;
  state justice-health policy. **OVA** is the defining workforce risk.
- **Defence/veteran:** Defence health contracts (Bupa prime); ANAO accountability audits; DVA-funded
  off-base networks; Open Arms.
- **Telehealth:** MBS telehealth item rules; AHPRA telehealth guidance (established relationship
  expectations); privacy.

---

## C. Funding levers

- **Dental:** mostly private out-of-pocket; **Child Dental Benefits Schedule** (~A$1,095–1,158/eligible
  child); limited state schemes.
- **Pharmacy:** **8CPA** dispensing + program funding; QCPP/QSPP-linked.
- **Optometry:** **MBS** (~94% bulk-billing — one of the highest); indexation; merged items (2025).
- **Correctional/defence:** **state government contracts** / **Commonwealth Defence & DVA** budgets —
  high-value, procurement-gated.

## D. Market fit & entry wedge (mixed per niche)

- **Pharmacy — compliance-led.** The **QCPP→QSPP transition** is a sector-wide, dated accreditation
  change every pharmacy must navigate. Lead with **ClinicBoss V-PHARM (QCPP/QSPP crosswalk) + F03
  Accreditation-Ready** + professional-service documentation. Channel: **banner groups** + **APP
  Conference** (Gold Coast, up to 7,500 attendees — Southern Hemisphere's largest).
- **Dental — education-led (IPC/radiation/CPD).** Lead with **IPC (U02) + Radiation Safety (S11) +
  CPD**, then **V-DENT** (sterilisation/reprocessing logs, radiation licensing, NSQHS day-procedure
  for sedation/surgical practices). Channel: **ADA congresses/ADX**, corporate dental groups.
- **Optometry — CPD-led.** Lead with CPD + teleoptometry-compliance training; light ClinicBoss CPD/
  policy layer. Channel: **O=MEGA** + banner HQs (Specsavers/OPSM).
- **Correctional/justice — OVA-led.** Lead with the **OVA flagship (F01) + V-OVA** — verbal abuse
  dominant, nurses main victims; RACGP prison-standards + NSQHS evidence layer. Channel: direct to
  state justice-health services.
- **Defence/veteran — partnership/tender-led.** The **2027 ADF HSC re-tender** is a live multi-
  billion-dollar opportunity; position as a **training + compliance subcontractor** to the prime,
  and DVA-network training (Open Arms-adjacent). Pursue via partnership, not direct.
- **Telehealth — attach.** Sell privacy/telehealth-compliance training + policy templates as an
  attach to any other pathway's customers using telehealth.
- **Cosmetic / IVF / imaging / pathology** — these consolidated, high-compliance niches have their
  own dedicated plan in **[Pathway 07](07-diagnostics-cosmetic-fertility.md)** (win-the-HQ channel +
  mandatory-accreditation/QMS wedge + 2024–27 regulatory shocks). Not duplicated here.

## E. ClinicBoss rollout & required functions

- Core (C2/C3/C4/C8) applies everywhere. Vertical packs:
  - **V-PHARM:** QCPP→QSPP crosswalk; professional-service program documentation (MedsChecks, DAAs,
    vax); cold-chain/SOP.
  - **V-DENT:** IPC/sterilisation reprocessing logs; radiation-apparatus licensing; NSQHS day-
    procedure crosswalk.
  - **V-HOSP (reuse)** for correctional health + day-procedure-style niches (NSQHS/RACGP prison
    standards crosswalk) + **V-OVA** for justice.
  - **Optometry/telehealth:** light C-stack (CPD logbook reuse from V-AH; policy templates).
- **Required net-new functions:** QCPP/QSPP crosswalk; dental sterilisation/radiation registers;
  RACGP-prison-standards crosswalk; teleoptometry/telehealth policy templates.

## G. Pricing & packaging

- **Banner/franchise deal:** **flat-unlimited/yr** or **per-site** sold to the banner HQ (pharmacy,
  optometry, corporate dental) — one sale, many sites; the most efficient outlier motion.
- **Independent practices:** low per-site/month + per-learner/yr education.
- **Correctional/defence:** government-contract pricing (per-site/enterprise, tender-gated).

---

## H. Channel & sales motion

- **Banner/franchise HQs** (Chemist Warehouse/Priceline/TerryWhite/Amcal; Specsavers/OPSM; corporate
  dental) — the highest-leverage outlier channel.
- **Peak-body conferences:** **APP** (pharmacy), **O=MEGA** (optometry), **ADA congresses/ADX**
  (dental).
- **Government:** state justice-health services (direct); Defence prime + DVA (partnership/tender).
- Sales cycle: pharmacy/dental/optometry via banner = medium; correctional/defence = long, tender-
  gated.

## I. Strategy to move through the stream

1. **Pharmacy first (M0–12):** ride the **QCPP→QSPP** transition with V-PHARM + F03 via a banner
   group and APP Conference — a clean, dated, sector-wide hook.
2. **Dental (M6–18):** IPC/radiation/CPD education → V-DENT into corporate dental groups; NSQHS pack
   for sedation/surgical practices.
3. **Justice (M9–18):** OVA flagship into state justice-health services (high, real need).
4. **Optometry (M12+):** CPD + teleoptometry compliance via O=MEGA/banner HQs.
5. **Defence (M18–30):** position for the **2027 ADF HSC re-tender** as a training/compliance
   subcontractor; build the relationship early.
6. **Telehealth (ongoing):** attach to every other pathway's telehealth-using customers.

## J. AI plays & guardrails

- **Plays:** AI accreditation-evidence assembly (QCPP/QSPP, NSQHS); policy generation; professional-
  service documentation/claiming helpers (pharmacy); CPD-reflection prompts.
- **Guardrails:** **dental/optometry imaging AI and pharmacy clinical-decision AI are TGA-regulated
  as SaMD by intended purpose** (clarified Feb 2026) — keep ClinicBoss on the compliance/
  documentation side, never diagnostic; onshore data; APP-compliant; PBS/Medicare data constraints;
  automated-decision disclosure by 10 Dec 2026.

## K. Risks & competitive notes

- **Entrenched niche PMS** (Dental4Windows/Centaur >80% corporate dental; Fred/Minfos pharmacy;
  Optomate/Sunix optometry) — integrate, don't replace; own compliance/accreditation/training.
- **Banner-group gatekeeping** — winning the HQ is high-leverage but concentrated risk; keep
  independent-practice self-serve alive.
- **Government procurement** (justice/defence) is slow and relationship-heavy — treat as long-horizon
  optionality, funded by the faster niches.
- **Data freshness** — day-hospital/private data stale. (Cosmetic/IVF/imaging/pathology follow-up
  research is complete — see Pathway 07.)
