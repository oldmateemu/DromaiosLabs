# Rung-3 Offer — "Safer Care Partnership" (annual arrangement)

The third rung in the offer ladder (`CUSTOMER_ACQUISITION_STRATEGY.md` §3). Where rung 1
(`workshop-package-rung1.md`) teaches and rung 2 (`offer-rung2-team-program.md`) embeds, this is an
**annual partnership** that keeps the standard alive across a whole year. It is the most predictable
revenue in the ladder, the lowest-friction renewal, and the relationship from which an earned
ClinicBoss design-partner conversation most naturally grows.

## The offer in one line
A 12-month partnership that keeps your safety-and-documentation standard alive — refreshed,
re-embedded after turnover, and extended across teams and sites — so "good" stays repeatable all
year, not just the quarter after a program.

## Why it exists (the gap rung 2 leaves)
A quarter-long program sets and embeds a standard. But care teams churn, rosters change, and
attention drifts. Without maintenance, even a well-embedded standard erodes over a year. The Safer
Care Partnership is the maintenance layer: scheduled refreshers, onboarding-into-the-standard for new
starters, and a standing point of contact so the standard doesn't quietly decay.

## Who it's for
- Organisations that completed a Safer Teams Program and want it to *last past the quarter*.
- Multi-site operators who need a single standard maintained across sites year-round.
- Leaders who'd rather have an ongoing partner than re-buy training reactively every time something
  slips.

## What the organisation can sustain across the year
- A **living standard** for escalation response and documentation that survives turnover.
- **New starters onboarded into the standard** as they join, not months later.
- **Quarterly refreshers** that keep habits sharp without a big re-training event.
- **Consistency across sites/shifts** maintained, not just achieved once.
- A **named partner** to call when something changes (a regulation, an incident, a new service).

---

## What's included (annual)
- **Quarterly refresher sessions** (per team or per site) — short, practical, reinforce the standard.
- **Onboarding-into-the-standard pack** so new starters join at the standard, run by the team.
- **Standard upkeep**: when a requirement or service changes, the shared standard and materials are
  updated, and the change is helped onto the floor (the "reaches the frontline" path).
- **A quarterly check-in** with the leader: a qualitative review of capability and consistency
  (described in plain language, not outcome claims) and the focus for the next quarter.
- **Priority access** for ad-hoc questions and a same-segment sounding board.
- **Practical tools/resources** (HIL/Skool) bundled where useful.

## Pricing posture
This is the recurring-revenue anchor. Make it a simple **annual fee**, billed monthly or quarterly,
with renewal as the default.
- **Single-site / single-team** — flat annual fee.
- **Multi-site** — annual fee scaled by sites, with a bundle rate.
- **Add-ons** — extra workshops, new-team programs, or event/webinar delivery at a member rate.

(Set actual numbers from cost/market. Per the guardrail, keep pricing in proposals, not public copy.)

Anchor on value honestly: a standard that *stays* consistent across a year and survives turnover —
without promising a specific reduction in incidents or audit findings.

## How it ladders
- **From rung 2:** offer the partnership at the week-12 review of the Safer Teams Program — the
  natural moment, when the standard is embedded and the question becomes "how do we keep it?"
- **Within the year:** add-on workshops and new-team programs are natural expansions inside the
  partnership.
- **To ClinicBoss (earned only):** a year-long partner with a clear operational-software pain is
  the ideal, most-trusted party to open a *problem-level* ClinicBoss design-partner conversation
  with — never as part of this pitch, never with feature or outcome claims.

## During the partnership (protect renewal + grow pipeline)
- Treat every quarterly check-in as a mini renewal conversation — show the standard is alive.
- Capture careful testimonials/case notes (`testimonial-and-case-note-template.md`) at milestones —
  no outcome numbers unless measured and approved.
- Expand within the account: new teams, new sites, extra workshops.
- For the right partners, open the earned, problem-level ClinicBoss conversation.
- Log in the cockpit sales function: partnership active → renewal date → expansion stage.
