# DromaiosEd Course Catalogue

> Shared reference for all market pathway plans. Pathways select courses from this catalogue.
> DromaiosEd is the **low-risk, fast-revenue, horizontal door-opener** into every segment:
> mandatory training is legally forced, recurring (mostly annual), non-SaMD, and budgeted.
> It is also where AI gives a small team disproportionate leverage today (rapid authoring +
> localisation) and where the clinician network adds credibility later (CPD accreditation +
> instructor-led delivery).

## Why education leads the "non-discriminatory" entry

Every Australian healthcare segment is legally required to evidence a near-identical
**mandatory-training stack**, plus a few sector modules. Selling that stack:

- carries **no TGA/medical-device risk** (it is training, not a device);
- has a **short sales cycle** (the buyer already has a budget line and a deadline);
- produces **recurring revenue** (annual refreshers, new-starter onboarding, high turnover);
- **pulls ClinicBoss through** (the training-evidence record is the natural first ClinicBoss
  module — see `clinicboss-module-catalogue.md` C3/C4).

This is the literal mechanism for entering all markets without discriminating: **one catalogue,
priced per learner/year, sold into eight segments**, with a handful of segment electives.

### Tier 1 — Flagship differentiators (Dromaios's edge)
Where Dromaios competes on quality and clinical credibility, not commodity price.

| Code | Course | Why it wins |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **F01** | **Occupational Violence & Aggression (OVA) / De-escalation** ⭐ | Dromaios's existing strength. Demand is surging — hospital assaults up ~44–60% across states (2015→2018); violence/assault workers'-comp claims +56% (2017–18→2021–22); >70% of aged-care workers report violence. Differentiate vs free government OVA training and generic providers on: **healthcare-specific scenarios, competency assessment, CPD accreditation, multi-state compliance, blended eLearning + instructor-led**. |
| **F02** | **Restrictive Practices & Behaviour Support** | Mandatory aged care + NDIS; state-specific authorisation. High-stakes, frequently failed in audits. |
| **F03** | **Accreditation-Ready series** | One course per standard: RACGP 5th ed, NSQHS 2nd ed, Strengthened Aged Care Quality Standards (7), NDIS Practice Standards, QCPP/QSPP. Teaches teams to *pass the audit*; the natural bridge to ClinicBoss C4. |
| **F04** | **Clinical Governance for Managers & Boards** | Boards are now economic buyers (new Aged Care Act elevates governing-body duties; ACCHO community boards; hospital clinical governance). Premium-priced. |
| **F05** | **Cultural Safety** | Mandatory-feel across AMS and increasingly all segments; gateway to the AMS relationship. Must be co-designed with First Nations clinicians (network). |

### Tier 3 — Credentialed / premium (clinician-network era)
Higher-margin, delivered/badged by the clinician network once revenue allows.

- Instructor-led OVA & PBS workshops (per-cohort/day pricing).
- CPD-accredited masterclasses (RACGP/college accreditation = moat).
- Train-the-trainer / in-house facilitator licensing.
- Bespoke org-specific competency assessment & sign-off services.

## Accreditation & credibility roadmap

1. **Now (AI-built, pre-network):** publish Tier 0/1/2 eLearning, AI-authored and AU-localised,
   mapped to each standard. Self-asserted quality; cite the standard each module satisfies.
2. **Early revenue:** obtain **CPD accreditation** (RACGP/ACRRM/college) for flagship courses —
   this is a durable moat competitors (and free government training) can't easily match, and it
   justifies premium pricing.
3. **Clinician network engaged:** clinicians **validate and badge** content, **deliver**
   instructor-led Tier 3, and act as **implementation consultants** — converting credibility into
   both quality assurance and a channel.

## Pricing model (detail in `00-master-strategy.md` §Pricing)

- **Per learner / year** subscription to the catalogue (benchmark ~A$25–60/learner/yr for
  AU healthcare LMS+content; price within this, undercut Ausmed/Altura on the long tail).
- **Per-organisation bundle** with ClinicBoss C3 ("Compliance + Capability").
- **Instructor-led OVA/PBS** per-cohort or per-participant-per-day (Tier 3, network-delivered).
- **Channel discount** mirrors NDS's etrainu model (e.g. peak-body member discount) to win
  association endorsement.

## AI leverage notes

- AI authors first drafts, generates assessment items, localises to state variations, and
  produces accessible formats — letting a near-solo team ship a full catalogue. **Human/clinician
  review before publish** for clinical accuracy and (later) CPD accreditation.
- Keep all learner data onshore and APP-compliant; disclose any automated decisioning (e.g.
  adaptive assessment) per the 10 Dec 2026 Privacy Act requirement.
